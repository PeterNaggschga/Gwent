Name:
Gwent - A Calculator

Short Description:
An app that simplifies playing Gwent using analog cards.

Description:
With Gwent - A Calculator you are able to play the card game Gwent from The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt using analog cards. Just print out some cards, download this app and start playing!
Usually a computer, console or smartphone calculates your points but with a real life opponent this calculation can get very complicated. Gwent - A Calculator is solving this problem!
Simply add some cards to your combat row, change the weather or activate a Commander's Horn and the app determines your points. Additionally it provides some useful features like random unit selection (useful when activating perk of Monsters faction).
